Account & Data Deletion

For the GreenThumb — Plant Care application by Ton Tsukanov

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data from the GreenThumb — Plant Care mobile application (the "Application").

How to request deletion — step by step

  1. Open the Application on your device and go to the Profile tab (bottom-right tab in the navigation bar).
  2. Tap Recovery key (or the equivalent option labelled with a key icon) to reveal your recovery key — a long random string that uniquely identifies your account. Copy it to your clipboard.
  3. Send an email to greenthumb.taunt861@passmail.net with:
    • Subject: Account deletion request
    • Body: paste your recovery key
  4. You will receive a confirmation reply within a reasonable timeframe (typically a few business days). Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from the backend within 14 days of the confirmation.

If you have uninstalled the Application and cannot access your recovery key, email the same address and describe what you remember (approximate date of first use, any device details). The developer will try to locate and delete your data on a best-effort basis.

What data will be deleted

  • All plant records you created (names, care schedules, notes, watering history)
  • All plant photos stored on the backend
  • Your user record (the recovery key entry in the users table)
  • Your push notification subscription (FCM token), if any

What data may be retained (and for how long)

  • Anonymous server access logs — generic HTTP access logs (timestamp, request path, IP address) are retained for up to 30 days by the hosting provider for security and troubleshooting purposes. These logs are not linked to individual user accounts and are automatically rotated.
  • Deletion request emails — the email you send may be retained by the email provider for record-keeping purposes, subject to the email provider's retention policy.
