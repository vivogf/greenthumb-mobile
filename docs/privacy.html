Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-18

This privacy policy applies to the GreenThumb — Plant Care app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Ton Tsukanov (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a free, open-source service. This service is intended for use "AS IS". The source code of the Application is publicly available at https://github.com/vivogf/greenthumb-mobile.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects only the information necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • Plant photos — images you take or select to attach to a plant. Stored on the Service Provider's backend so they are available across sessions.
  • Plant data — names, care schedules, notes, watering dates that you enter manually.
  • Device identifier (recovery key) — an anonymous random string generated on first launch and stored securely on your device. It is used as your account key instead of an email and password. The Service Provider cannot recover this key if you lose it.
  • Push notification token — issued by Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and stored on the Service Provider's backend only if you explicitly enable watering reminders in the app settings. Used solely to deliver reminders to your device.

The Application does not collect your name, email, phone number, age, gender, contacts, location, microphone input, or any analytics about your usage (screens visited, time spent, crash reports). No user-tracking SDK is integrated.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data.

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Service Provider does not use your data for marketing or send promotional messages.

Third Party Access

The Application relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Services — used to deliver push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) when you enable watering reminders. Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo — used to deliver over-the-air updates to the Application's JavaScript bundle. No personally identifiable information is transmitted to Expo. Expo Privacy Policy

The Service Provider does not sell, rent, or trade your information. The Service Provider may disclose information only in the following cases:

  • as required by law (subpoena or similar legal process);
  • when necessary to protect the Service Provider's rights, investigate fraud, or comply with a government request;
  • with trusted service providers who process data on the Service Provider's behalf (e.g. cloud hosting) and are contractually obligated to adhere to this privacy policy.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all data collection by the Application by uninstalling it using the standard uninstall process available on your mobile device or via the Google Play Store. Push notifications can also be disabled individually from the Application's profile screen without uninstalling.

Data Retention and Deletion

The Service Provider retains your data for as long as you use the Application. Because the Application uses an anonymous recovery-key-based authentication, the Service Provider cannot identify your data without the recovery key.

If you would like your data to be deleted, please contact the Service Provider at greenthumb.taunt861@passmail.net with your recovery key, and the Service Provider will remove the associated data in a reasonable timeframe.

Children

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact the Service Provider at greenthumb.taunt861@passmail.net so the Service Provider can take the necessary actions.

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. All communication between the Application and the backend is encrypted via HTTPS. The recovery key is stored on your device using the platform's secure keystore (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain) and is never transmitted in plain text.

You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of those changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy.
